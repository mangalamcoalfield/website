## Environmental clearance context (PARIVESH / MoEFCC)

Amlabad's historic underground workings sit within BCCL's Eastern Jharia Area, which has been the subject of environmental-clearance proceedings under the EIA Notification, 2006, administered through PARIVESH (the MoEFCC online clearance system). BCCL collieries in Jharia have historically been clustered for environmental clearance (the "cluster" EC approach).

> **Important:** Any environmental clearance, Terms of Reference (ToR), EIA/EMP or public-hearing record relied on for publication must be matched to the **specific EC that authorises Mangalam's current Amlabad operations** and quoted from the actual PARIVESH/EC document — not inferred from BCCL's older area/cluster ECs. Confirm the exact EC number, date, granting authority, sanctioned capacity and validity from the PARIVESH proposal record before publishing any clearance figure. See Flags.

Canonical places to pull the authoritative EC/EIA record:
- **PARIVESH** (MoEFCC clearance portal): https://parivesh.nic.in/ and https://cpc.parivesh.nic.in/
- **Environment Clearance archive:** https://environmentclearance.nic.in/

## What is safe to state publicly (page-ready)

These statements are supportable from public sources and contain no confidential data:

- Mangalam Coalfield Private Limited (incorporated 2024, registered in Kolkata) is the Mine Developer & Operator reviving the historic Amlabad underground pits in Eastern Jharia, Jharkhand.
- The revival programme covers Pit No. 1, 2, 3 and 4, through phased revival, development and dewatering.
- Amlabad lies in the Jharia coalfield, India's most important prime coking-coal region.
- The company runs two commercial lines from the block: coal, and coal bed methane (CBM).

## Flags — confirm against internal records (accuracy only)

1. **Reserve figure.** A public BCCL figure of *~45.94 million tonnes* is associated with the historic "Amlabad Project." This is a BCCL public number, **not** Mangalam's DPR. Do **not** present it as Mangalam's reserves and do **not** enrich it with internal data. Recommend: omit from marketing copy; if used anywhere, attribute to BCCL public records and confirm relevance.
2. **Exact EC details.** EC number, date, granting authority, sanctioned capacity (MTPA) and validity for the operation Mangalam runs — pull and verify directly from PARIVESH before publishing any clearance claim.
3. **Pit No. 2 status.** Confirm the current, publishable phase of Pit No. 2 (the public record clearly describes Pits 1, 3 and 4).
4. **Workforce / capacity numbers.** Any headcount or capacity stat on the site must be a curated, publication-approved figure — never a live ops figure and never an internal production number.
5. **Administrative geography.** Amlabad is variously described against Bokaro (Chandankiyari) and the Dhanbad-based Eastern Jharia Area; confirm the precise wording the company wants for address/footer use.
