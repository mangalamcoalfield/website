What we do

Two operations, one operator.

Through the Amlabad block in Eastern Jharia, Mangalam runs two commercial lines — underground coal, and the coal bed methane held within the same seams.

01
Coal · Mining

Reviving the Amlabad pits

Restoring historic underground workings — Pit No. 1 in revival, Pit No. 3 under development, Pit No. 4 in dewatering — to power India's industrial growth through responsible coal.

02
Coal Bed · Methane

Energy within the seam

Commercially capturing the methane held inside the coal seams — a cleaner energy stream from the same block, put to use rather than vented to the atmosphere.

Safety & compliance

The condition for permission to operate.

Underground coal mining carries real risk. We treat statutory safety as the baseline, not an afterthought.

  • Qualified statutory officials across all DGMS-mandated positions
  • Maintained statutory registers and periodic equipment testing
  • Gas monitoring, ventilation discipline, and roof-support standards
  • Medical examinations and competency tracking for the workforce
Zero
compromise on the standards that keep people safe underground.
