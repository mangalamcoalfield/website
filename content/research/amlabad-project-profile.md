## What is safe to state publicly (page-ready)

These statements are supportable from public sources and contain no confidential data:

- Mangalam Coalfield Private Limited (incorporated 2024, registered in Kolkata) is the Mine Developer & Operator reviving the historic Amlabad underground pits in Eastern Jharia, Jharkhand.
- The revival programme covers Pit No. 1, 2, 3 and 4, through phased revival, redevelopment, development and dewatering.
- Amlabad lies in the Jharia coalfield, India's most important prime coking-coal region.
- The company runs two commercial lines from the block: coal, and coal bed methane (CBM).

## Flags — confirm against internal records (accuracy only)

1. **Reserve figure.** A public BCCL figure of *~45.94 million tonnes* is associated with the historic "Amlabad Project." This is a BCCL public number, **not** Mangalam's DPR. Do **not** present it as Mangalam's reserves and do **not** enrich it with internal data. Decision: keep off the site.
2. **Workforce / capacity numbers.** Any headcount or capacity stat on the site must be a curated, publication-approved figure — never a live ops figure and never an internal production number.
3. **Administrative geography.** Amlabad is variously described against Bokaro (Chandankiyari) and the Dhanbad-based Eastern Jharia Area; confirm the precise wording the company wants for address/footer use.
