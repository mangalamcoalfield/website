# Mangalam Cares

## Eyebrow
Mangalam Cares

## Heading
The work above ground matters too.

## Intro
Beyond production, Mangalam invests in the people and the environment around the mine. A coalfield is also a community — the families, the workforce, and the land around the pits. Mangalam Cares is how we account for our responsibility to all three.

Everything shown here is **Mangalam's own** contribution. Completed work is shown with evidence; planned work is clearly labelled as planned, so the line between what we have done and what we intend to do is never blurred.

## Community

We engage with and support the communities around Amlabad — local welfare, and investment in the skills and safety awareness of the people who live and work near the mine.

## Environment & sustainability

Responsible mining does not end at the pit. Our environmental commitment covers the land we operate on and the resources we use: plantation and green-cover contributions in the mine vicinity, responsible water management, and dust-control measures around our operations. Our coal bed methane line is part of this picture too — putting seam methane to use rather than venting it.

## How we report this

- **Completed** initiatives are published with supporting evidence.
- **Planned** initiatives are labelled as planned until delivered.
- We showcase only Mangalam's own contributions — not the statutory reclamation or activity of other operators in the coalfield.
