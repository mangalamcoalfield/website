# Mangalam Coalfield — Website Content Brief (for Cowork)

**Goal:** Write and organise all the written content for the new Mangalam Coalfield website, and compile the publicly available project data. Deliver everything as clean markdown, organised by page, ready to drop into the site or Supabase.

**Voice:** Confident, institutional, plain-language — the authority on Indian coal mining. Grandeur through substance and clarity, not hype. Indian English.

## Hard rules (publish-filter — non-negotiable)
- Use **only** information already public through an official channel (the company's own site, Ministry of Coal / DGMS / PARIVESH, Environmental Clearance / EIA / EMP, public hearings).
- **No confidential data:** no DPR contents (reserves, geology, mine-plan economics, production schedules), no site-specific gas/methane readings, no internal production figures. If unsure, leave it out.
- Vet any public figure against internal records for **accuracy only** — never add internal detail to a public number.
- Use these facts consistently: Mine Developer & Operator; reviving the historic Amlabad pits — **Pit No. 1, 2, 3 and 4** — in **Eastern Jharia, Jharkhand**; **two commercial lines: coal and coal bed methane (CBM)**; incorporated 2024; honours **Late Shree Gopal Agarwal, Chairman Emeritus**.

## Produce, in priority order

**1. Page copy** (one markdown file per page):
- **Home** — hero line, the two-business-line blurbs, short section intros.
- **About / Leadership** — company story; **Chairman Emeritus** tribute to Late Shree Gopal Agarwal (legacy: rich legacy, resilience, shared values; a new generation upholding trust, integrity, excellence); current leadership.
- **Operations & Capabilities** — Coal Mining (the four-pit revival) + Coal Bed Methane; the capabilities (Mine Development, Underground Operations, Electrical & Mechanical, Statutory Compliance).
- **Safety & Compliance** — DGMS, statutory positions, registers, gas/ventilation, medical examinations.
- **Mangalam Cares** — completed CSR (with evidence) + planned (labelled); environment & sustainability.
- **Careers intro + Contact copy.**

**2. Knowledge-hub articles** ("How Coal Mining Works") — ~600–1000 words each, plain-language for a general + professional audience; each ends with version-dated links to the canonical source (DGMS / Ministry of Coal / Coal Mines Regulations 2017 / Mines Act):
- Bord & pillar underground coal mining, explained.
- Mine ventilation & methane management (the science).
- Coal grades & the National Coal Index.
- DGMS & the rules of the mine (regulatory overview).
- *(Then a backlog list of future article ideas.)*

**3. CSR write-ups** — a short entry per completed/planned initiative: title, what it is, impact, and a note of which photo/evidence goes with it.

**4. Public Amlabad project profile** (research task) — compile the publicly available project facts from PARIVESH / EC / EIA and the company's own site into a clean reference for the Operations and About pages. Flag anything that should be double-checked against internal records for accuracy.

**5. Content calendar** — a simple monthly plan for ongoing knowledge-hub articles and the "How Coal Mining Works" video series, noting which article pairs with which video.

## How it connects
Deliver as markdown, organised by page/section. Page copy → MDX files in the website repo; jobs / CSR / knowledge entries → rows in Supabase (via Supabase Studio). Keep a short running index of what's written and what's pending.
