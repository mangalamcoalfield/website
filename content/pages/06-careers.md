## Intro
From mining engineers to electrical and statutory roles — join a company that runs its operations to standard. Mangalam Coalfield is reviving the historic Amlabad pits in Eastern Jharia, and we are building the team to do it safely, compliantly, and well.

Coal mining is skilled work, and we treat it that way. Whether you hold a statutory qualification, an engineering trade, or you are early in your career and ready to learn underground, there is a place here for people who take the standard seriously.

## Why Mangalam

- **Standards you can stand behind.** Statutory compliance and safety are how we operate, not how we market.
- **Real responsibility.** Clear roles, qualified officials, and structured reporting on every shift.
- **A company being built.** Join early and help shape how the Amlabad revival is run.
