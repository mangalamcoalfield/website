## What we do — two operations, one operator

**Section intro:**
Through the Amlabad block in Eastern Jharia, Mangalam runs two commercial lines — underground coal, and the coal bed methane held within the same seams.

**Line 01 — Coal · Mining**
*Reviving the Amlabad pits.* Restoring historic underground workings across Pit No. 1, 2, 3 and 4 — through revival, development and dewatering — to power India's industrial growth through responsible coal.
