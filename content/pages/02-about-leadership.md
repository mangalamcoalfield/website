## The company

Mangalam Coalfield Private Limited is a Mine Developer and Operator focused on reviving the historic Amlabad pits in Eastern Jharia, Jharkhand. Incorporated in 2024 and headquartered in Kolkata, the company brings disciplined engineering and statutory rigour to underground coal — and to the coal bed methane held within the same seams.

Amlabad is among the oldest names in the Jharia coalfield. Our purpose is straightforward: to bring these proven underground workings back into safe, compliant, productive operation — Pit No. 1, 2, 3 and 4 — through phased revival, development and dewatering, and to do so to the letter of the Coal Mines Regulations and the Mines Act.

We measure ourselves not by ambition alone, but by the standards we hold on every shift: people brought up safely, registers maintained, equipment certified, and coal won responsibly to power India's industrial growth.

## What guides us

- **Safety first, always.** Statutory safety is the condition for permission to operate — not a box to tick.
- **Compliance as a baseline.** We operate to the Coal Mines Regulations and the Mines Act, with qualified officials in every role.
- **Engineering rigour.** Shift-by-shift discipline in development, production, and maintenance.
- **Responsibility above ground.** Care for the workforce and the environment around the mine.
